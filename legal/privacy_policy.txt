**Privacy Policy for Burger Byte Studio**

_Last updated: November 14, 2024_

---

**Introduction**

Welcome to Burger Byte Studio, a digital and physical goods provider managed by Osyra Renaya ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy outlines the methods, purposes, and limitations of any data collection that may occur when you engage with our services, specifically regarding orders of digital and physical products. We are committed to respecting your privacy and ensuring that any data you share with us is managed responsibly.

---

**1. Data Collection Practices**

At Burger Byte Studio, we do not engage in any form of automated data collection. No information about you, your device, or your browsing habits will be gathered through automated means when you visit our website or contact us. 

Data collection will only occur in the following circumstances:

1.1 **Digital Product Orders**  
   - When you order a digital product, we will collect small amounts of personal information. This will generally include contact details, such as your name and email address, and any other information necessary to complete your order.
   - This information is collected manually and only with your voluntary consent. We refer to our customers who have provided information as "Members."

1.2 **Physical Product Orders**  
   - If you order a physical product, a physical shipping address will be required to fulfill the order.
   - This information will only be collected with your explicit consent, ensuring you have full control over any data you provide.

**Note:** Data collected for both digital and physical orders is entered into our systems manually and only with the client’s full knowledge and willingness to share such data.

---

**2. Purpose of Data Collection**

Burger Byte Studio collects information solely for the purpose of processing orders and delivering products. Specifically:

- Contact data collected for digital product orders is used solely to manage and deliver the requested products to you.
- Address information provided for physical orders is used only for shipping purposes.

No information provided by you will be used for any purposes outside the order fulfillment process.

---

**3. Limitations on Data Usage**

We have implemented the following strict limitations on how your data will be used:

3.1 **No Advertising or Marketing**  
   - As of November 14, 2024, we do not use your contact information to promote new products, services, or offers. You will not receive promotional emails, newsletters, or any form of advertising communication based on the information you provide.

3.2 **No Sharing with Third Parties**  
   - We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for any purpose, including advertising, data analysis, or marketing.

3.3 **Data Access Control**  
   - The information you provide will be accessible only to Osyra Renaya and Burger Byte Studio staff responsible for managing your order. We prioritize data security and ensure that all personal information remains confidential and secure.

---

**4. Data Retention**

Personal information collected for orders will be retained only as long as necessary to complete your transaction, handle any related customer support requests, and meet any legal or financial obligations.

After this period, we will either delete or securely store any retained data in compliance with relevant data protection laws.

---

**5. Your Rights**

As a valued Member of Burger Byte Studio, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Access** – You can request to know what data we have collected about you.
- **Correction** – You can ask us to correct any inaccurate or outdated information.
- **Deletion** – You may request that we delete your data after order completion.
- **Data Portability** – Upon request, we can provide a copy of your personal data in a structured format.

To exercise these rights or ask questions, you can reach us through the contact details provided below.

---

**6. Security of Your Information**

We employ reasonable security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no online or offline data transmission or storage can be guaranteed as completely secure, and we encourage you to share information cautiously.
