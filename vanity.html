About Vanity

Vanity has been a passion project since about 2017.
The project has never been serious and never will be.
The concept: "How ridiculous can a discord bot be?"

Main Features

Vanity's current version utilizes an advanced, uncensored language model (LLM) to generate responses, ensuring a nuanced and accurate communication style. Integrated with a sophisticated voice model, this iteration goes beyond textual interaction by audibly delivering responses on Discord. This dual capability not only enhances engagement but also adds a dynamic layer to the platform's functionality. Users benefit from a more immersive experience where spoken responses complement textual ones, enriching the overall interaction and usability of Vanity significantly.

Minecraft Features

In historical versions, Vanity had the capability to connect to any vanilla Minecraft server and interact with players using commands sent to her in discord. Plans were in place to advance its functionality to procedurally understand player actions and offer assistance. However, the Minecraft 1.13 update rendered the bot incompatible, leading to its functionality breaking down. With the popularity of the new update among players who were testing the bot, I redirected development efforts towards implementing different features. Unfortunately, this update, with its promising advancements, became a lost chapter in Vanity's development history.
