Glitched (2013)
"Glitched" introduces Nala and her sister Lela, a former soldier. The story explores their daily lives and the lingering effects of Lela's military past. Lela must make a critical decision about her injured friend Pony, hinting at a potential return to military service. The narrative delves into themes of family, personal growth, and the aftermath of war.

Ever Diamond (2007)
In "Ever Diamond," Tom's life takes a dramatic turn when he discovers a mysterious ring with almost unlimited powers. As he and his friends delve deeper into the ring's secrets, they encounter a series of challenges and revelations. The story unfolds with intricate plotlines involving magic, war, and the quest for the Holy Time Charm. Characters like Tom, Pony, and Lela navigate through a world where ancient weapons, enchanted items, and the Ever Diamond play pivotal roles. The narrative explores themes of loyalty, sacrifice, and the consequences of wielding immense power, all while blending elements of fantasy and adventure. The story reaches a climax with a battle against a dragon, where the true nature of the Ever Diamond and the characters' destinies are revealed.
