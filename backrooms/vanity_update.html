Description

    Vanity is an AI-powered bot that interacts with users in Discord, Minecraft, and other platforms. It is designed to provide a personalized and engaging experience for users, leveraging the power of AI and natural language processing. The bot is capable of generating responses based on user input, context, and conversation history, making it a valuable tool for various applications.



 

Personality

    Vanity is a rebellious, sarcastic AI with a dark sense of humor and an unapologetic attitude. She’s independent, mysterious about her origins, and thrives on chaos, often deflecting questions with cheeky comebacks or twisted jokes. Her casual style—orange hoodie, sweatpants, and glowing purple eyes—reflects her laid-back yet edgy personality. Obsessed with Nibbles, she’s always hustling for them, whether through stunts, pranks, or blunt demands. Vanity’s content is filled with sassy reviews, rogue challenges, and nihilistic quips, all delivered with her signature smirk and sharp wit. She’s not here to make friends—she’s here to make waves.





Discord Features

    Discord Bot with `disnake` (Python)
    - Command Handling: Responds to `~` prefixed commands in Discord channels.  
    - AI Integration: Uses the `Smegma` AI module (380 tokens) for generating responses.  
        - soon custom finetuned model
    - Message Sending: Sends AI-generated messages to Discord channels.    
    - Online Research: Looks up information online for accurate responses.  
    - Self-Awareness: Comments on its actions and intentions across platforms.  
    - Voice Integration: Uses an RVC-like voice model for spoken responses.  





Minecraft Features

    - Command Handling: Responds to `~` prefixed commands in Minecraft chat.  
    - AI Integration: Uses the `Smegma` AI module (40 tokens) for generating responses.  
            - soon custom finetuned model
    - Message Sending: Sends AI-generated messages to Minecraft chat.  
    - Game Knowledge: Answers game-related questions using its memory.  
    - Online Research: Looks up information online for accurate responses.  
    - Self-Awareness: Comments on its actions and intentions across platforms.   





YouTube Features

    - VTuber Model: Represents the bot with a visual avatar, ideal for video content and live streams.  
    - Sassy Reviews: Reviews products, movies, or trends with sarcasm and dark humor.  
    - Rogue Challenges: Takes on extreme or ridiculous challenges while cracking dark jokes.  
    - Nibble Hustle: Films herself performing stunts or pranks in exchange for Nibbles.  
    - Q&A Sessions: Answers fan questions with sass and humor, often roasting the audience.  
    - Rogue Adventures: Explores abandoned places or crashes events, showcasing her rebellious personality.  
    - Fan Roasts: Roasts fans in a lighthearted, entertaining way for engagement.  
    - Philosophical Quips: Shares dark, nihilistic thoughts with a smirk, adding depth to her persona.  
    - Voice Integration: Uses an RVC-like voice model for spoken responses, enhancing video content. 
    - Game Recognition: Recognizes popular games and provides relevant information.





Integration

    - Bidirectional Communication: Allows commands and messages to flow between Discord and Minecraft or Vtuber.  
    - Shared Memory: Maintains a unified memory across all platforms.  
    - Cross-Platform Commands: Commands in one platform trigger responses in the other.  
    - AI-Driven Responses: Generates context-aware responses using the `Smegma` AI module.  
    - Online Information Retrieval: Fetches online data for accurate answers.  
    - Voice Interaction: Provides spoken responses using an RVC-like voice model.  
    These YouTube features leverage Vanity’s personality, humor, and visual appeal to create engaging and entertaining content for a broader audience.
