3D Prints
3D printing is a type of additive manufacturing technology that creates objects by adding material layer by layer until the desired shape is achieved. This is in contrast to subtractive manufacturing methods, such as cutting or drilling, which remove material from a larger block or sheet. 3D printing has a wide range of applications, including prototyping, product design, medical implants, and more.
